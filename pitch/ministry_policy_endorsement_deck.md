## Slide 1 - Why This Matters Now
### Title
A Governance Execution Gap Is Putting Democratic Trust at Risk

### On-slide copy
- AI-enabled disinformation now scales faster than institutional response workflows.
- Ministries face a coordination problem, not only a detection problem.
- Delayed triage and inconsistent response quality create avoidable public-risk exposure.

### Speaker notes
- Open with mission-level framing: protect democratic integrity, public trust, and lawful digital governance.
- Position urgency in operational terms: speed, consistency, accountability.
- Transition: We built a practical response system that closes this execution gap.

## Slide 4 - How It Works in Practice
### Title
From Alert to Actionable Briefing in Minutes

### On-slide copy
1. Submit suspicious content as raw text or media URL.
2. System extracts claims and entities and evaluates synthetic risk, coordination signal, and democratic-impact severity.
3. Case dossier returns narrative summary, legal exposure flags, and policy-linked recommended actions.
4. High-risk incidents are surfaced into a prioritized escalation queue.

### Speaker notes
- Walk through one realistic scenario: false polling station change narrative.
- Emphasize human-in-the-loop: system supports decisions, does not replace officials.

### Visual direction
- Four-step horizontal flow with screenshot placeholders from home, case detail, and queue views.
